Data Security & Privacy Policy

Version
v3.1
Owner
Michael Chen
Effective Date
Dec 1, 2024
Category
IT & Security

1. Purpose

This policy establishes the standards for protecting company and customer data, ensuring compliance with applicable privacy regulations, and maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who have access to company information systems or handle sensitive data.

3. Key Requirements

Password Security

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all systems (minimum 12 characters)
  • Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all accounts
  • Never share login credentials with anyone
  • Change passwords immediately if compromise is suspected

Data Protection

  • Encrypt sensitive data both at rest and in transit
  • Only access data necessary for your job responsibilities
  • Never store sensitive data on personal devices
  • Use approved cloud storage solutions only

Incident Response

  • Report suspected security incidents within 1 hour
  • Do not attempt to investigate security incidents on your own
  • Contact the Security Team immediately at security@company.com
  • Preserve all evidence and do not delete any files

4. Training & Compliance

All employees must complete annual security awareness training. Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
