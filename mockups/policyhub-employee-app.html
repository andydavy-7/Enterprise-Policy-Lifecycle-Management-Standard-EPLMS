Who Can Work Remotely?
~3 min read

Our remote work policy is designed to provide flexibility while maintaining productivity and team collaboration. This section outlines who is eligible and the approval process.

Eligibility Criteria

All full-time employees who meet the following requirements are eligible to request remote work arrangements:

  • Tenure: Completed at least 6 months with the company
  • Performance: "Meets Expectations" or higher on most recent performance review
  • Role suitability: Job responsibilities can be effectively performed remotely
  • Manager approval: Direct manager supports the remote work arrangement

Remote Work Options

Employees can choose from three remote work arrangements:

  • Hybrid (1-2 days/week): Work from home up to 2 days per week
  • Hybrid (3-4 days/week): Work from home 3-4 days per week, requires manager approval
  • Full remote: Work remotely 5 days per week, requires VP approval
