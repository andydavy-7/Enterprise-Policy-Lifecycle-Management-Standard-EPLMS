1. Purpose

This policy establishes the standards for protecting company and customer data, ensuring compliance with applicable privacy regulations, and maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who have access to company information systems or handle sensitive data.

3. Key Requirements

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all systems (minimum 12 characters)
  • Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all accounts
  • Never share login credentials with anyone
  • Encrypt sensitive data both at rest and in transit
  • Report suspected security incidents within 1 hour
  • Complete annual security awareness training
