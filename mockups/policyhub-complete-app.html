Remote Work Policy v2.0

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Owner: HR Department

1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for remote work arrangements to support employee flexibility while maintaining operational effectiveness and security standards.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees who are eligible for remote work arrangements as determined by their role requirements and manager approval.

3. Eligibility

Employees may be eligible for remote work if they:

  • Have been with the company for at least 6 months
  • Have a role that can be performed remotely
  • Have demonstrated strong performance and self-management
  • Have appropriate workspace and technology at home

4. Guidelines

Remote employees are expected to:

  • Maintain regular working hours and availability
  • Respond to communications within established timeframes
  • Attend required meetings (virtual or in-person)
  • Ensure secure handling of company data and equipment

Remote Work Policy v2.0

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Owner: HR Department | Last Updated: 2 hours ago

Policy Content

What's New in Version 2.0
This policy has been updated with important changes
  • Added guidelines for international remote work
  • Updated eligibility requirements (6 months tenure)
  • Clarified communication expectations

1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for remote work arrangements to support employee flexibility while maintaining operational effectiveness and security standards.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees who are eligible for remote work arrangements as determined by their role requirements and manager approval.

3. Eligibility

Employees may be eligible for remote work if they:

  • Have been with the company for at least 6 months
  • Have a role that can be performed remotely
  • Have demonstrated strong performance and self-management
  • Have appropriate workspace and technology at home

4. Guidelines

Remote employees are expected to:

  • Maintain regular working hours and availability
  • Respond to communications within established timeframes
  • Attend required meetings (virtual or in-person)
  • Ensure secure handling of company data and equipment

5. International Remote Work

NEW: Important Update

International remote work is now permitted for up to 30 calendar days per year. This requires prior approval from your manager, HR, and Legal departments due to tax and employment law implications.

To request international remote work:

  1. Submit request at least 30 days in advance
  2. Obtain manager approval
  3. Complete HR and Legal review process
  4. Ensure you have reliable internet and can work during team hours

6. Equipment and Security

The company provides necessary equipment for remote work. Employees must:

  • Use company-provided devices for work activities
  • Connect via VPN for accessing company resources
  • Keep software and security patches up to date
  • Report any security incidents immediately
