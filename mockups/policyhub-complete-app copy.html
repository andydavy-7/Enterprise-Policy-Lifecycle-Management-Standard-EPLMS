Remote Work Policy v2.0

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Owner: HR Department

1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for remote work arrangements to support employee flexibility while maintaining operational effectiveness and security standards.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees who are eligible for remote work arrangements as determined by their role requirements and manager approval.

3. Eligibility

Employees may be eligible for remote work if they:

  • Have been with the company for at least 6 months
  • Have a role that can be performed remotely
  • Have demonstrated strong performance and self-management
  • Have appropriate workspace and technology at home

4. Guidelines

Remote employees are expected to:

  • Maintain regular working hours and availability
  • Respond to communications within established timeframes
  • Attend required meetings (virtual or in-person)
  • Ensure secure handling of company data and equipment
