Remote Work Policy

v2.0
Sarah Johnson
Dec 14, 2025
Dec 18, 2025

1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for employees who wish to work remotely, either on a full-time or part-time basis. It aims to provide flexibility while maintaining productivity, collaboration, and security standards.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time employees who have completed their probationary period and whose roles are suitable for remote work arrangements.

3. Policy Statement

Our company supports flexible work arrangements that benefit both employees and the organization. Remote work is a privilege granted based on job requirements, performance, and business needs.

Employees approved for remote work must maintain the same level of productivity and availability as if working in the office. All company policies, including those related to confidentiality, data security, and professional conduct, continue to apply in remote work settings.

4. Eligibility

To be eligible for remote work arrangements:

  • Employee must have completed probationary period
  • Role must be suitable for remote work
  • Employee must have demonstrated strong performance
  • Manager approval required

5. Guidelines

Remote workers are expected to:

  • Maintain regular working hours and be available during core business hours
  • Have a dedicated workspace with reliable internet connection
  • Participate in all required meetings via video conference
  • Respond to communications promptly
  • Protect company data and use approved security measures
